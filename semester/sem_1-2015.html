Literature Review

  • Cognitive rehabilitation
  • Serious game for rehabilitation


Some of the interesting information

  • Stroke-related medical costs have increased while the numbers of clinical treatment sessions have declined.
  • Previous studies using video games as the primary intervention have found improvements in reaction time, short-term and long-term memory, attention, and spatial ability.
  • There are many kinds of developed rehabilitation devices from commercial console to webcam camera.
     
  • There are a number of ways to assess cognitive function, and some of them are available in Thailand's rehabilitation institute.
  • There are two alternating bi-annual international conferences in VR rehabilitation.
  • There remain unresolved questions about the translation of VR rehabilitation into practice.
  • Well-designed game based on therapeutic principles will empower patients to be involved in their rehabilitation.

EMG Analysis

  • EMG data were collected twice to investigate the effect of sliding mass of Gyro-Roller.
  • For the first record, the mass was moved and stayed still.
  • The second record, the mass was being moved continuously at constant rate during recording.


EMG Analysis

  • The analysis of first dataset has been carried out but hardly able to see any difference between move and not-move of Gyro-Roller mass
  • The second dataset is still in processing, collaborating with Titipat A.

We represent EMG for each task as summation of principle component which are principle components and their corresponding weight. The signal said to be correlated if the principle weight are correlated. Therefore, we find correlation between them.
